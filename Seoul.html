Centru cultural in Seoul




The architectural project located in the Gangnam district of Seoul, near a river, is a multifunctional cultural center designed to connect the two separated areas of the district. The center features an alpine wall, offices, a versatile hall, and other amenities. Furthermore, it includes a bike path, emphasizing the project's aim to promote a range of activities and encourage accessibility. The project was designed using a Grasshopper script, showcasing the design team's technical expertise and efficient use of technology.
