Art, specifically painting and sculpture, has a direct connection with architecture. I have been painting since the age of 10 and have over a decade of experience with oil painting. I acknowledge that this experience has greatly assisted me during my architectural studies, particularly with regards to color theory, artistic sensibility, composition, aesthetics, and the creation of architectural sketches, textures, and more. Thus, I consider it important to showcase these skills within my portfolio.
